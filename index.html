Get Started

Download & Begin the Grind

You'll need an existing High Five client. Download our system patch, replace the folder, and log in. Three steps back to where it all began.

  • 1
    Download the System Patch
    Our custom system folder — replaces the original client files to connect to Ophelia.
  • 2
    Extract & Replace
    Extract the archive and copy the System folder into your Lineage II installation directory, replacing the existing one.
  • 3
    Launch & Connect
    Run l2.exe and log in. No registration needed — accounts are created automatically on first login. Welcome to Ophelia.
